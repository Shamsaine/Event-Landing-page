Our Journey

Why We Started Alteturia

We founded Alteturia because we saw a critical gap in tech education. Aspiring professionals were learning outdated frameworks, struggling to connect with real industry mentors, and feeling isolated in their journey. Meanwhile, companies were struggling to find skilled talent.

Our mission became clear: create a bridge between learners and the industry by providing structured education, expert mentorship, and a supportive community. We combine practical hands-on training with real-world project experience and AI-powered learning tools to prepare professionals for the jobs of today and tomorrow.

Today, Alteturia serves as a comprehensive ecosystem where aspiring tech professionals can learn, grow, connect, and launch successful careers.
